Our Applications

SharpSpeak

SharpSpeak is an English as a Second Language (ESL) learning app built around the concept of comprehensible input. It personalizes each learner’s journey based on their language level and provides immediate, tailored feedback to help students achieve fluency more effectively.

ButtonStopwatch

ButtonStopwatch is a sleek and simple stopwatch that operates using volume buttons. Designed for users who want to track time without looking at the screen, it’s perfect for workouts, presentations, or any task where visual attention is limited.

NutritionAssistant

NutritionAssistant helps users achieve their health goals by analyzing their daily routine and fitness objectives. It calculates their optimal macronutrient needs, builds customized meal plans, and supports them through the cooking and serving process. It's especially useful for couples or families needing help with food portioning.
